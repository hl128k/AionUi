# 04-creative-gradient — Creative Gradient Brand

## Style Overview

Gradient purple color scheme design, suitable for brand launches, company introductions, startup pitches, and other brand narrative scenarios

- **Scene**: Brand launches, company introductions, startup pitches, brand stories
- **Mood**: Innovative, professional, elegant, cutting-edge, quality
- **Tone**: Cool tones, gradient colors, purple palette
- **Industry**: Tech, design, consulting, cultural creativity, internet
